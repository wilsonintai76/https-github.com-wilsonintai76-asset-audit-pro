Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 16, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Inspect-able. We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information that you provide directly to us, such as when you log in using your institutional Google account. This includes:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (if available)
  • Institutional affiliation (based on email domain)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our application.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our application.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our application.
  • Develop new products, services, features, and functionality.
  • Communicate with you for administrative purposes.
  • Prevent fraud and ensure system security.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access. However, no internet transmission is ever 100% secure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Supabase for authentication and database services. Your data is stored securely in accordance with their privacy standards.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the PKS Asset Management Unit.
